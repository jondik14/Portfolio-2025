Share Economy Website Design

Company Share Economy
Role UX / UI Designer
Expertise Interaction Design
Year 2023

Overview

The Share Economy project involved designing an interactive and accessible platform for peer-to-peer resource sharing. The focus was on clear communication of value and creating a high-trust interface for users.

Visual Language

We developed a vibrant and modern visual language that reflects the dynamic nature of the sharing economy. High-contrast colors and bold typography ensure accessibility and clarity.
